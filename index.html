About Us

GoParkNow helps you find parking spaces in your locality. It has a “Search Near Me” feature which lets you find parking spaces within a radius of 2KM from you. GoParkNow also has a search by location feature using which you can do a location wise search for parking spaces. GoParkNow gives detailed info about each parking space like photo of the location, Parking Charges, Operating time and gives google map directions to each parking space.
